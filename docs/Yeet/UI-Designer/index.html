UI-Designer

The designer is the place where your App will be developed.

Sidebar​

In the sidebar either pages and popups can be selected and edited in the "Overview"-Tab or components can be added in the "Components"-Tab. Components can be added by either clicking on them or by draggin and dropping them inside the workarea.

Workarea​

It is possible to view all the components that belong to your page or popup in the workarea. By clicking on your components, you will be able to edit them in the

that will pop up. The workarea also contains rulers at the top and the left, with an eye-icon in the top left corner. By clicking on the eye-icon guide and measure-tools for the workarea can be activated. Guides can be added by dragging and dropping them from one of the rulers. They can be deleted by dragging a guide and dropping it back on one of the rulers.

Propertybar 1​

The propertybar 1 is located inside the workarea and is dynamically placed relative to the selected component.

Propertybar 3​

The propertybar 3 is located at the right side of the viewport and provides specifications for the selected component.

Breadcrumb-Menu​

In the bottom of the ui-designer right below the workarea, the Breadcrumb-Menu can be found. In this menu the structural hierachy of the selected component and its parents will be displayed. This might be helpful if your selected component is inside one or more containers without any margin or padding separating the component from its parent.

Topbar​

In the top of the designer the topbar can be found. In the topbar, files can be saved, undo and redo can be called, breakpoints can be chosen and various other functions like the preview can be called.
